Research Notes: Color Theory in Digital Art

Abstract:
This document explores the psychological impact of color choices in digital artwork and how artists can use color theory to enhance emotional storytelling.

Key Findings:
1. Blue tones evoke calm and introspection
2. Orange creates energy and warmth
3. High contrast increases visual impact
4. Color temperature affects viewer engagement

Applications for Digital Artists:
- Use cool colors for contemplative scenes
- Warm colors for action and energy
- Strategic contrast for focal points
- Temperature shifts for emotional transitions

References:
- Itten, Johannes. "The Art of Color" (1961)
- Adobe Color Theory Guidelines (2023)
